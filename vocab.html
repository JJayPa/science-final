Vocabulary

radio waves : One of electromagnetic waves and used for long distance communication.

Wavelength : Distance between two identical points on neighboring.

Electro-Magnetic Spectrum : A range of all types of radiation that has electric and magnetic field and are a wave.

frequency : number of waves that pass a fixed point in a certain amount of time.

hertz, gigahertz : Unit of frequency. 1 billion hertz = gigahertz.

photons : A particle representing a quantum of electromagnetic radiation.
